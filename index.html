FAQ

Quick answers to common HVAC questions.

Do you offer emergency HVAC repair?
Yes. Call the number on this page. If we cannot pick up, leave a voicemail and request a text-back.
Can you come out today?
Same-day availability depends on the schedule and parts. We will confirm the soonest slot by phone or text.
Do you give pricing over the phone?
We can share common ranges, but accurate pricing usually requires diagnosis. We provide options and upfront pricing before work.
What should I do if I smell gas or burning?
If you suspect a gas leak, leave the area and contact your gas utility or emergency services. For burning smells, shut off the system if safe and call.
Do you service heat pumps?
Yes. We handle heat pump troubleshooting, repairs, maintenance, and replacement guidance.
How do I request service without calling?
Use the request form on this page. It is designed to capture the minimum details needed to schedule quickly.
